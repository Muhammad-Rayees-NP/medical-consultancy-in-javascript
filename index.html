Welcome to Medical Consulting

Medical Consulting Group offers comprehensive medical business solutions and strategies tailored for medical practices, ambulatory surgery centers (ASCs), and medical corporations. With a team of diverse strategic consultants, we assist clients in the development, management, and promotion of their medical practices

With over three decades of experience, we have provided consultancy across a wide range of medical surgical specialties, with a primary focus on ophthalmology and eye care. Whether you operate a single-specialty or multi-specialty facility, our expertise is at your service to define objectives, create detailed business plans, and effectively execute them to achieve your aspirations
